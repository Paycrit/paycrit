Short version: We collect only what we need to respond to your enquiries and deliver our services. We never sell your data. You can ask us to delete everything at any time. For questions, email chandan_ravi@yahoo.com.

This Privacy Policy explains how Paycrit (“we”, “us”, “our”) collects, uses, stores, and protects information when you use our website at paycrit.github.io/paycrit (the “Site”) or engage us for Managed Services.

1. What Information We Collect

a) Information you give us directly

When you fill out our contact or enquiry form, we collect:

  • Full name
  • Business email address
  • Business or store name
  • Monthly processing volume (self-reported range)
  • Current payment provider(s)
  • Nature of your enquiry or challenge
  • Any additional context you provide in the message field

For Managed Services engagements, we may also collect additional business details necessary to perform the agreed work (e.g. provider statements, business category, transaction volumes). This information is used solely to deliver the service.

b) Information collected automatically

Our hosting platform (GitHub Pages) may log basic technical data including:

  • IP address
  • Browser type and version
  • Pages visited and time on site
  • Referring URL

We do not use cookies, advertising pixels, retargeting tags, or cross-site tracking scripts of any kind.

2. How We Use Your Information

Information you submit is used only to:

  • Respond to your enquiry within a reasonable timeframe
  • Schedule and conduct discovery calls or Managed Services engagements
  • Send relevant follow-up about your specific request (you may opt out at any time)
  • Issue invoices and maintain business records for Managed Services clients
  • Improve our service based on common questions and challenges we hear from merchants

We will never:

  • Sell, rent, or trade your data to any third party
  • Use your data for advertising or profiling
  • Send unsolicited commercial messages (spam)
  • Share your business details with any payment provider without your explicit consent

3. How We Store Your Information

Contact form submissions are routed through Make.com (an automation platform) and stored in our Airtable workspace. Both services implement appropriate technical and organisational security measures. Make.com and Airtable are US-based services.

For Managed Services clients, we may also retain email correspondence and relevant documents for business record purposes.

We retain your information for as long as needed to deliver the requested service, plus a reasonable record-keeping period (typically 2 years). You may request deletion at any time — see Section 7.

4. Sharing Your Information

We only share your information with the following service providers, strictly to operate our business:

  • Make.com — processes and routes contact form submissions
  • Airtable — stores and manages contact records and client information
  • Email (Yahoo Mail) — used to communicate with you directly

We do not sell, license, or transfer your personal data to any third party under any circumstances. We do not share your information with payment providers or any financial institutions without your explicit, prior consent.

5. Third-Party Services on the Site

The Site uses the following third-party resources that may collect limited technical data:

  • Google Fonts — loads typefaces from Google’s servers (subject to Google’s Privacy Policy)
  • Tailwind CSS CDN — provides styling from a content delivery network
  • Brandfetch CDN — serves payment provider logos on our comparison pages

These services may log your IP address as part of serving web assets. We have no control over their data practices. No sensitive personal data is shared with these services.

6. Children’s Privacy

The Site is intended solely for business users aged 18 and over. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18. If you believe a minor has submitted information to us, please contact us immediately at chandan_ravi@yahoo.com and we will delete it promptly.

7. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data, consistent with applicable Canadian privacy law (PIPEDA) and other applicable regulations:

  • Access — request a copy of all information we hold about you
  • Correction — ask us to correct inaccurate or outdated information
  • Deletion — request that we permanently delete your data from all systems
  • Portability — request your data in a portable, machine-readable format
  • Objection — object to specific uses of your data where you feel it is unwarranted
  • Withdraw consent — opt out of any email communications at any time by replying “unsubscribe”

To exercise any right, email us at chandan_ravi@yahoo.com. We will respond within 30 calendar days.

Data protection laws in these countries may differ from those in your home jurisdiction. By using the Site, you consent to such transfers. We take reasonable steps to ensure your data is handled securely regardless of where it is processed.
