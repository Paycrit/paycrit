Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 1, 2025

Short version: We collect only what we need to respond to your enquiries. We never sell your data. You can ask us to delete everything at any time.

This Privacy Policy explains how Paycrit ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our website at paycrit.github.io/paycrit (the "Site") or contact us for Managed Services.

1. What Information We Collect

a) Information you give us directly

When you fill out our contact form on the Services page, we collect:

  • Full name
  • Business email address
  • Business or store name
  • Monthly processing volume (self-reported range)
  • Current payment provider(s)
  • Nature of your enquiry
  • Any additional details you provide in the message field

b) Information collected automatically

Like most websites, our hosting platform (GitHub Pages) may log basic technical information including:

  • IP address
  • Browser type and version
  • Pages visited and time spent
  • Referring URL

We do not use cookies or cross-site tracking scripts. We do not run advertising pixels or retargeting tags.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information you submit to:

  • Respond to your enquiry or question
  • Schedule and deliver a discovery call or managed services engagement
  • Send you relevant follow-up communications about our services (you can opt out at any time)
  • Improve the Site and our service offering based on common questions and pain points

We will never use your information to:

  • Sell or rent it to third parties
  • Send unsolicited commercial emails (spam)
  • Profile you for advertising purposes

3. How We Store Your Information

Contact form submissions are processed through Formspree (formspree.io) and stored in our Airtable workspace. Both services are based in the United States and maintain appropriate security measures.

We retain your information for as long as needed to deliver the service you requested, plus a reasonable period for business records (typically 2 years). You may request deletion at any time — see Section 7.

4. Sharing Your Information

We share your information only with the following categories of service providers, strictly for delivering our service:

  • Formspree — form processing and email delivery
  • Airtable — contact management and internal CRM
  • Google Workspace — email communications

We do not sell, license, or trade your personal data to any third party under any circumstances.

5. Third-Party Services on the Site

The Site uses the following third-party resources:

  • Google Fonts — fonts are loaded from Google's servers (subject to Google's privacy policy)
  • Tailwind CSS & Chart.js CDN — loaded from jsDelivr and Tailwind's CDN
  • Brandfetch CDN — used to display payment provider logos

These services may collect limited technical data (e.g. IP address) in the course of serving assets. We have no control over their data practices.

6. Children's Privacy

The Site is intended for business users and is not directed at children under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has submitted information to us, please contact us immediately.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access — request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Correction — request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion — request that we delete your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Portability — request your data in a machine-readable format
  • Objection — object to certain uses of your data
  • Withdraw consent — opt out of any email communications at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us via the contact form and we'll respond within 30 days.

8. International Users

If you are accessing the Site from outside the United States, please note that your information may be transferred to and processed in countries where data protection laws may differ from those in your country. By using the Site, you consent to such transfers. We implement appropriate safeguards to protect your information during such transfers.

9. Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. However, no data transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we'll update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this page periodically. Continued use of the Site after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests related to this Privacy Policy, please reach out via our contact form.
